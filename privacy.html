NeuroLeap Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("NeuroLeap", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at neuroleap.io.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated automatically through your use of the platform:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay — we never store card details)
  • Learning activity data (lessons completed, time-on-task, quiz responses)
  • Device and browser information
  • Communications you send us

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is used solely to provide, improve, and personalize our platform:

  • To power the NeuroSync™ adaptive learning engine
  • To issue and verify certificates
  • To provide customer support
  • To send course updates and important notices
  • To improve our curriculum and platform features

AI & LEARNING DATA

Your learning data — including responses, time-on-task, and engagement signals — is used exclusively to personalize your learning experience through NeuroSync™. This data is never sold, never shared with advertisers, and never used to train models for other users without explicit consent.

All AI processing is performed on encrypted data. Raw learning signals are anonymized after 90 days for aggregate research purposes only.

DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (under strict data processing agreements)
  • With hiring partners — only with your explicit opt-in consent when you apply via Career Launch
  • When required by law or legal process

SECURITY

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit), regular security audits, and follow OWASP best practices. Certificates are secured via the Polygon blockchain. We report breaches within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time from your account settings. You may also contact us at privacy@neuroleap.io for any data-related requests. We respond within 30 days.

COOKIES

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management, and optional analytics cookies (which you can disable in settings). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

CONTACT US

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@neuroleap.io or write to us at 91 Springboard, Koramangala, Bengaluru 560095.
