FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How does the AI personalization work? +
Our NeuroSync™ engine tracks 200+ learning signals — response time, error patterns, engagement depth — and rebuilds your learning path every session. No two learners ever see the exact same curriculum.
Can I switch courses or cancel anytime? +
Yes. All plans are month-to-month. You can switch courses, pause, or cancel anytime from your dashboard with no cancellation fees or penalties.
Are the certificates recognized by employers? +
Our certificates are blockchain-verified and recognized by 240+ companies including Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Razorpay, CRED, and more. They are mapped to NASSCOM and IEEE skill standards.
Do you offer EMI or scholarships? +
Yes. We partner with Bajaj Finserv and NBFC lenders for 0% EMI on 3, 6, and 12-month plans. We also offer need-based scholarships — contact admissions@neuroleap.io to apply.
What is the job placement guarantee? +
Eligible graduates (those who complete 100% of curriculum + capstone project) receive our placement guarantee: if you don't land a job within 6 months of completion, we refund your subscription fees.
